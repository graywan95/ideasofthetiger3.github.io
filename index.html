See how easy it is to get scammed?

Be very cautious before scanning any QR code, especially ones posted by random people or businesses you don't know.

Scammers can create fake QR codes that lead to malicious websites designed to steal your login credentials, credit card info, or install malware on your device.

Avoid scanning codes on flyers, posters, or public surfaces that anyone could have tampered with.
